Disabling GlobalProtect

k, so this is a little confusing, ill make it as simple as possible

Setup:

1. Restart your computer, and on the login screen, click on wifi and turn it off.

2. Click on the "Sign in options" text, under the password box. Then click the key.

3. Login as usual

Actual thing:

1. Click the arrow in the corner of your screen and click on the GlobalProtect icon

2. Click on the 3 lines in the corner

3. Go to "Connections" and click the plus

4. Type in "localhost:8080"

5. Click the collierschools connection and then click the trash icon.

6. Go back to the icon and click connect

If you get "unable to connect", you are done!
